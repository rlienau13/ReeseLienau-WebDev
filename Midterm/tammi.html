Tammi



Tammi! A planar, rotating puzzle with three “centers” around which interchangeable pieces revolve.
Three laser-cut plexiglass layers are held in place by aluminum rods while rubber feet protect the base of the puzzle.
Its bright and unique design leaves the solved state of this puzzle resembling the subtractive color theory.
To learn more about how it was made, check out my instagram (also linked in the top right corner of this webpage): @reeselienaumetalarts
