Penelope



Penelope! A decagonal prism puzzle crafted with the help of a bandsaw, a belt sander, and a LOT of Apoxie Sculpt!
To learn more about how it was made, check out my instagram (also linked in the top right corner of this webpage): @reeselienaumetalarts
This puzzle was selected from over 230,000 works of art to win a Scholastic Gold Medal Award on the National level, putting it among the top 0.03% of submitted works!
